Example 1 Basic - Flash Enabled On Fixed Width element

$(".testBox.eg1").qlip().on('copy', blink);
Copy "He's climbin' in your windows" to your clipboard

Example 2 Fluid Width + Flash Enabled

$(".testBox.eg2").qlip({updateOnWindowResize:true}).on('copy', blink);
Copy "He's snatchin' your people up" to your clipboard

Example 3 No Flash

$(".testBox.eg3").qlip({swf:null}).on('copy', blink);
Copy "Hide your kids, hide your wife" to your clipboard
